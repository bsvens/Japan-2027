Three nights to land, settle in, and hit the version of Tokyo everyone pictures — old temple gates by day, neon streets by night.

Shibuya & Shinjuku

二 · ACT TWO Hakone

Two nights, complete change of pace. Mountains, a lake, a hot spring — the reset between Tokyo and Kyoto.

Day 4 · Mar 8

Lake Ashi & Mt. Fuji

A scenic lake cruise with Fuji in the background, weather permitting. Slow, quiet, completely different from Tokyo.

Day 5 · Mar 9

Onsen & Ryokan

A traditional inn with a natural hot spring bath and a multi-course dinner. No itinerary — this day is the point.

三 · ACT THREE Kyoto

Four nights — the traditional side of Japan. Thousands of orange gates, a bamboo forest, lantern-lit streets. The birthday lands here.

Day 7 · Mar 11

Fushimi Inari

Thousands of orange gates climbing the mountain. The most photographed stop of the whole trip.

Day 8 · Mar 12

Arashiyama Bamboo Grove

Towering bamboo forming a green tunnel, plus a monkey park overlooking the city.
