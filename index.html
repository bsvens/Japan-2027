The Itinerary

Nine nights, three acts.

Tokyo's energy, Hakone's quiet, Kyoto's tradition — and Anicka's birthday landing right in the middle of the best part.

一 ACT ONE Tokyo Mar 5–8
Day 2 · Mar 6

Senso-ji Temple

Tokyo's oldest temple, a giant red lantern gate, and a market street leading right up to it.

Day 2–3

Shibuya & Shinjuku

The neon, the crossing, the izakaya alleys — this is the postcard shot of Tokyo at night.

二 ACT TWO Hakone Mar 8–10
Day 4 · Mar 8

Lake Ashi & Mt. Fuji

A scenic lake cruise with Fuji in the background, weather permitting. Slow, quiet, completely different from Tokyo.

Day 5 · Mar 9

Onsen & Ryokan

A traditional inn with a natural hot spring bath and a multi-course dinner. No itinerary — this day is the point.

三 ACT THREE Kyoto Mar 10–15
Day 7 · Mar 11

Fushimi Inari

Thousands of orange gates climbing the mountain. The most photographed stop of the whole trip.

Day 8 · Mar 12

Arashiyama Bamboo Grove

Towering bamboo forming a green tunnel, plus a monkey park overlooking the city.

Evenings

Gion

Old wooden streets, lantern light, Kyoto's traditional district — our birthday dinner setting.
